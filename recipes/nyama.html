Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)

One of the meals that is always served whenever there is a barbecue, or whenever friends and family come together during football matches, for Friday evening meals.

Ingredients

  • Goat or beef meat, cut into bite-sized chunks -- 2 pounds
  • Oil -- 3 tablespoons
  • Warm water -- 2 cups
  • Kosher or sea salt -- 2 tablespoons

Steps

  1. Prepare your grill and have it hot.
  2. Toss the meat with the oil, then thread it on skewers.
  3. Stir the salt into the warm water until it is fully dissolved.
  4. Grill the skewered meat, basting it occasionally with the salt water, until it is cooked to your desired doneness.
  5. Remove the meat from the skewers and serve with kachumbari salad and ugali.
