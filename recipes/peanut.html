Peanut Butter Balls (Power Balls)

Kids love these easy, quick, and energy-filled treats.

By dance4eva

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • ½ cup milk powder
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ½ cup chocolate chips (Optional)

Steps

  1. Stir peanut butter, milk powder, honey, and chocolate chips together in a bowl until evenly mixed. Scoop up about 1 1/2 tablespoon of mixture per serving, and shape into round balls. Arrange on a plate. Freeze until set, about 30 minutes.
